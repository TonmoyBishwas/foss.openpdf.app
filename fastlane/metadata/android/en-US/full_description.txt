OpenPDF is a completely free and open-source PDF app — a full alternative to
proprietary readers, including the features they usually lock behind a
subscription. There are no ads, no accounts, no subscriptions, and no tracking.

OpenPDF has no internet permission at all: your documents never leave your
device.

Features:

* View: fast rendering, pinch and double-tap zoom, continuous scroll and
  page-by-page modes, night and sepia reading modes
* Navigate: table of contents, bookmarks, go-to-page, full-text search
* Listen: read aloud with on-device text-to-speech
* Annotate (saved into the PDF): highlight, underline, strikethrough, sticky
  notes, freehand drawing, text boxes, shapes, and an eraser
* Fill & sign: fill interactive form fields and place your signature
* Organize: rotate, reorder, duplicate, delete, and extract pages; merge and
  split PDFs
* Create: make PDFs from images, camera scans (with a black & white filter), or
  typed text
* Protect: add or remove an AES-256 password, and view or edit document metadata
* Share and print

OpenPDF only opens files you explicitly choose, and only writes where you tell
it to. Nothing is uploaded, indexed, or collected.
