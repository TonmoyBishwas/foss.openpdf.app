OpenPDF

A completely free, open-source PDF reader & editor for Android

Download Source code Privacy policy
No ads. No accounts. No tracking. No internet permission. Everything happens on your device — your documents never leave it.

What it does

  • View: fast rendering, zoom, continuous & page-by-page, night & sepia modes
  • Navigate: outline, bookmarks, go-to-page, full-text search
  • Read aloud with on-device text-to-speech
  • Annotate (saved into the PDF): highlight, underline, strikethrough, notes, drawing, text boxes, shapes
  • Fill & sign forms; draw and place a signature
  • Organize: rotate, reorder, delete, extract, merge, split
  • Create from images, camera scans, or text
  • Protect with AES-256; view & edit metadata
