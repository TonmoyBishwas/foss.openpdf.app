OpenPDF Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026

OpenPDF collects nothing, sends nothing, and stores nothing about you.

No data collection

OpenPDF does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data. There are no analytics, no advertising, no crash reporting, and no accounts.

No internet access

OpenPDF is built without the INTERNET permission. The app is technically incapable of sending your documents — or anything else — over the network. Everything happens locally on your device.

Your files

  • Documents you open, annotate, or create are processed entirely on-device.
  • OpenPDF only accesses files you explicitly choose through Android's document picker (the Storage Access Framework). It does not scan or index your storage.
  • Files you create or save are written only to the locations you pick.

Permissions

Camera (optional): used only if you choose "Scan with camera" to create a PDF from photos. Images are captured through your device's camera app and never leave the device. If you never scan, this permission is never used.

Children

OpenPDF does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes & contact

Any changes to this policy will be published here and in the app's public source repository. Questions? Open an issue at github.com/TonmoyBishwas/foss.openpdf.app/issues.
