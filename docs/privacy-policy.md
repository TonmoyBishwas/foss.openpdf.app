# OpenPDF Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-06_

OpenPDF is a free, open-source PDF reader and editor for Android.

## The short version

**OpenPDF collects nothing, sends nothing, and stores nothing about you.**

## No data collection

OpenPDF does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information or usage
data. There are no analytics, no advertising, no crash reporting, and no
accounts.

## No internet access

OpenPDF is built **without the `INTERNET` permission**. The app is technically
incapable of sending your documents — or anything else — over the network.
Everything happens locally on your device.

## Your files

- Documents you open, annotate, or create are processed entirely on-device.
- OpenPDF only accesses files you explicitly choose through Android's document
  picker (the Storage Access Framework). It does not scan or index your storage.
- Files you create or save are written only to the locations you pick.

## Permissions

- **Camera** (optional): used only if you choose "Scan with camera" to create a
  PDF from photos. Images are captured through your device's camera app and
  never leave the device. If you never scan, this permission is never used.

## Children

OpenPDF does not collect data from anyone, including children.

## Changes

Any changes to this policy will be published in this file in the app's public
source repository: https://github.com/TonmoyBishwas/foss.openpdf.app
